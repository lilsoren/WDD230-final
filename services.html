Amenities

Temple Inn & Suites comes with many free amenities to make your stay better than ever. Before diving into our list, we want to make sure that the basics are covered. Every restroom in our hotel comes with basic personal care items such as shampoo, conditioner, lotion, soap, toothpaste, disposable combs, blow dryer, shaving cream, disposable razors, and bathrobes. In each hotel room there will additionally be an ironing board, steamer, set of scriptures, and a hot chocolate maker. Each guest is granted access to free parking, free breakfast, free internet access, access to the fitness center, and access to the swimming pool. Guests are invited to participate to attend activities that occur throughout the week such as musical performances, karaoke, dances, and magic shows. Guests are also invited to wonder the gardens and enjoy the beauty of nature if they are looking for a relaxing, spiritual space.

Reception Details

If you and your spouse are looking for the perfect wedding reception venue, you may stop looking. Our hotels provide a convenient and stress-free opportunity to seamlessly plan your special day. The first thing about a reception is the food. Our hotels give you the option to book a catering crew through our kitchen staff or to bring in outside catering/food of your choosing. In addition, your guests can easily book rooms with us, so there is no need to worry about your guest getting lost or being late. Event coordinators are available upon request to plan, set up, and help in any way that they can. We offer the option of an inside or outside reception as well as access to tables, chairs, and a full set of speakers for music and dancing.
